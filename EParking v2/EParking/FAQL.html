Frequently Asked Questions

You ask, we answer.

1. How do I become a member of eparking?

To become a member of eparking you must register your email address, a username of your choice, your personal data (check privacy policy for more information about your personal data).

2. Can eparking use the data / information I have entered?

Eparking may use part or all of the data / information you have entered for statistical purposes and to improve the services provided, in accordance with the Privacy Policy.

3. What do I need to do to be able to rate a parking that appears in eparking?

You must be a member of eparking and post your review through the contact us page.

4. How do I make a reservation?

You must be a member of eparking and you can choose the parking that serves you best, through the Book Now page. From there you select the time period you wish to park your vehicle and finally make the online payment.

5. What is the pricing policy and does it apply to all cooperating parking lots.

All our cooperating parking lots follow the same pricing policy. 25 € per day, 5 € per hour and 0.1 € per minute.

6. If I am a parking owner, how do I become a partner?

To become a member of the eparking partner family, all you have to do is contact us through the contact form and we will contact you as soon as possible, to be informed about the criteria and the process of joining your business on eparking.
