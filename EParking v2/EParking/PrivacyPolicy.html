Privacy Policy

The protection of your Personal Data is important to us!

At eparking.com we want you to know and have control over what data you share with us and what we do with it. This text (called the Privacy Policy) explains in simple words but also clearly what data we collect and how we use it. Its purpose is to help you stay informed and decide what data you want to share with us and how.

The eparking company located at 80 Karaoli ke Dimitriou Street, Pireas 185 34, is legally responsible for processing the personal data of the visitors of the website www.eparking.com and the users of the eparking application (registered and not), at in the context of the provision of services for the promotion, classification and comparison of product prices, as well as in the context of the provision of booking services.

1. What is Personal Data?

'Personal Data' means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person; an identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identifier such as a name, an identification number, location data, an online identifier or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that natural person.

2. What is processing of Personal Data?

'Processing of Personal Data' means any operation or set of operations which is performed on personal data or on sets of personal data, whether or not by automated means, such as collection, recording, organisation, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction.

3. Is it mandatory to provide your Personal Data?

The provision of your Personal Data to us is necessary to achieve the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy and our Platform’s Terms of Service. If you refuse to provide the required information, it will be impossible to achieve the main purpose of collecting the Personal Data, and it will make it impossible for us to fulfill the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy and our Platform’s Terms of Service.

4. What Personal Data do we collect from you?

We collect only the absolutely necessary Personal Data, which are appropriate and clear for the intended purpose. This Personal Data includes the following:
-Full name (first name, last name)
-Email address
-Billing address
-Banking data, specifically information related to your bank products (cards) such as, card number, product holder name, card expiration date and CVV code.
-Vehicle/s plate number/s

5. We strive to ensure your Personal Data is secure

We understand the importance of protecting your privacy and we will ensure your Personal Data is safeguarded and held securely in accordance with our obligations under the applicable legislation. Appropriate technical and organizational measures have been implemented to ensure personal information is protected and to prevent your Personal Data from being lost, used, accessed in an unauthorized way, altered or disclosed. We also limit access to your personal information to those employees, contractors and other third parties who have a business need to know. This access will be granted on a confidential basis and processing of the information will only take place subject to our instructions. We also share your Personal Data with third party service providers, that process your Personal Data as Data Processors on our behalf and in accordance with our orders.
